We use Google AdMob to display banner ads. In EEA/UK we request consent via Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). On iOS we may request AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) permission for IDFA. Data is stored in Firebase and encrypted at rest; communications use HTTPS. Analytics data is typically retained for 14–26 months. Account and purchase metadata are retained as required by law.

Deleting the app removes local data stored on your device. Server-side data (such as purchase metadata and analytics) remain until deleted upon request via email.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us to delete it.
