In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

The app also offers an optional one-time purchase (Lifetime access) which grants permanent access to all premium features without a recurring subscription.

Subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Free trials (3 days) convert to paid unless canceled before the trial ends. Prices and availability may vary by country.

Manage or cancel your subscription:
• iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
• Android: Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions

Refunds are handled by Apple/Google under their policies. iOS users can restore purchases via the in-app “Restore Purchases”.

Advertising & Consent

We use Google AdMob to display banner ads. In EEA/UK we request consent via Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). On iOS we may request AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) permission for IDFA. If you do not consent, we show only non-personalized ads. You can change your ad consent in the app settings at any time.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in Firebase and encrypted at rest; communications use HTTPS. Analytics data is typically retained for 14–26 months. Account and purchase metadata are retained while your subscription is active or as required by law.

Deleting the app removes local data stored on your device. Server-side data (such as purchase metadata and analytics) remain until deleted upon request via email.

Nákupy v aplikácii a predplatné

Aplikácia taktiež ponúka voliteľný jednorazový nákup (Doživotný prístup), ktorý zaručuje trvalý prístup ku všetkým prémiovým funkciám bez opakovaného predplatného.

Predplatné spracúva Apple App Store alebo Google Play. Predplatné sa automaticky obnovuje, ak nie je zrušené aspoň 24 hodín pred koncom. 3-dňová skúška sa zmení na platené predplatné, ak nie je včas zrušená. Ceny a dostupnosť sa líšia podľa krajiny.

Správa/zrušenie predplatného:
• iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
• Android: Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions

Refundy spravujú Apple/Google podľa svojich pravidiel. Na iOS je možné obnoviť nákupy cez „Restore Purchases“.
